// QUESTIONS

The quick answers.

Who can join?
Any teen aged 13–19, beginner or experienced. No prior hackathon experience needed — just curiosity and a willingness to build.
Do I need a team?
Nope. Come solo and form a team on-site during team matching, or bring up to 3 friends. Teams can be 1-4 people.
What does it cost?
TripleH is completely free to attend, including meals, snacks, and swag — thanks to our sponsors.
What should I bring?
A laptop and charger, a valid ID, any hardware you want to tinker with, and a sleeping bag if you plan to crash during the overnight hours.
